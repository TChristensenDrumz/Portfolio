About Me

Terrapin Christensen, born in Dubuque, Iowa, is a professional drummer currently based in Glendale, California. Terrapin has preformed with groups including the Colburn Adult Jazz Combo. Terrapin is trained in a wide range of musical genres including jazz, rock, funk, latin, hip-hop, reggae, metal, and pop. Terrapin also has a year of experience in JavaScript and is currently enrolled in the UCLA Extension Coding Bootcamp
